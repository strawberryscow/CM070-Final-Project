How to Read this Dashboard

This dashboard predicts whether a selected asset is likely to move UP or DOWN over the next 3 days. The prediction is generated using machine learning models trained on historical market data.

  • Prediction:The expected direction of the asset price.
  • Confidece:Model probability for the prediction.
  • SHAP Features:Shows which market indicators influenced the prediction.
  • GA Strategy:A trading strategy optimized using a genetic algorithm.
Important Note

Predictions are based on historical market patterns and do not guarantee future performance.
